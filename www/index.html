Purpose of AirAware
AirAware translates atmospheric physics into simple signals your body may notice, helping you stay aware and better prepare for changing conditions.

AirAware estimates environmental body-load potential from air-density movement, elevation context, and optional personal signals such as BP, pulse, and sensitivity. It is wellness guidance only, not medical advice.
Core terms
PLD = Practical Load Differential. How much the air environment is shifting relative to recent conditions.
Calculated using changes in atmospheric pressure, air density, and density altitude across time intervals.
DA = Density Altitude. A practical measure of how the air behaves based on temperature, pressure, and humidity — not just your elevation.
DA Burden = Density Altitude minus actual elevation, showing how much heavier or lighter the air may feel than expected.
24h Shift = density-altitude change compared with about 24 hours ago.
30m Shift = short-term density-altitude movement over recent minutes.
Mountain Effect = air feels thinner / lighter.
Valley Effect = air feels heavier / denser.
Air behavior
Air pressure = the weight of air above a location. Higher pressure usually means heavier, denser air; lower pressure usually means lighter, thinner air.
Air density = how thick or thin the air is. It is shaped by pressure, temperature, and humidity.
Temperature / humidity = warm air tends to be lighter, cold air tends to be denser, and humid air can be lighter than dry air at the same pressure.

Score Explanation

Temporary score guide
Higher scores indicate greater environmental variability and body-load potential.

The score reflects more noticeable shifts in air density over time, including within the hour and across about 24 hours. These changes may be influenced by weather patterns, storm fronts, pressure changes, temperature shifts, humidity shifts, or seasonal transitions.

This score does not diagnose any condition. It reflects environmental changes that may influence how current air conditions feel to the body, especially for sensitive individuals.
